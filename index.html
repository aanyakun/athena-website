Things to do when your bored!

Hello and welcome to my website!! In this website I will be providing you guys with a list of fun things to do when your bored :)

A little bit about me before you start reading, my name is Aanya and I'm a rising junior! I created this project as one of my submissions for the Hack Club Athena Awards. I love running, coding, and spending time with my friends and family! I hope you guys find this helpful for days when you are just bored and have nothing to do :))

You can also favorite the activities you like the most by selecting them :)). Have fun!

  1. A lot of times our body needs Vitamin D to feel good and be more productive! Take time out of ur day to go for even as little as a 15 minute walk to feel encouraged and soak in some sunshine!

  2. Reading books is so much fun and a great way to pass time :). All you have to do is pick a book that you like, curl up in bed, and enjoy!!

  3. Baking a sweet treat is the perfect way to unwind and have some fun! You can bake anything you like, whether its brownies, cookies, tiramisu, pies, or more!! There are tons of great recipes online as well or you can get creative :))

  4. Painting is a super fun idea, especially when you are bored. You can paint on a canvas, tote bags, t-shirts, and much much more!!

  5. Listening to podcasts can be so helpful and motivating. Take time out of your busy day to relax and simply listen to a podcast of your choice.

  6. Running is a great way to get some exercise and is honestly so peaceful when you get the hang of it :). Play your favorite song, go outside, and have a good time!

  7. Volunteering is a great way to give back to your community and is also fun especially in the summer when there's no school to keep you busy!

  8. Having a rom-com movie marathon is such a fun way to enjoy the day! Find a comfy spot, get some snacks, pick out your fav rom-com movies, and enjoy :)

  9. This is honestly such an underrated way to pass time, but it is so much fun! Looking through all your old memories can be so enjoyable and is something I personally love to do in my freetime <33

  10. Game nights can be so nice when you are with your friends/family!! It's a great way to feel happy and have a good time without having to use screentime

  11. Hiking is a great way some exercise and also enjoy the outdoors! Find a local trail and go have some fun in nature :)

  12. Scrolling on Pinterest is such a fun way to get inspiration and creative ideas!

  13. Learning a new language can be so much fun! You can sign up for online classes and it's a great way to expand your knowledge + have fun

  14. Learning how make a website can be so much fun and can be so useful for the future, especially if you have an online business. You can get started by using Youtube tutorials and platforms like Weebly :))

  15. Having a spa day at home is such a fun way to relax and unwind! You can do your favorite face mask, take a relaxing bubble bath, and more!

  16. Writing a letter to your future self can be a fun activity and great for you to reflect on in the future!!

  17. Pilates can be such a fun way to exercise and is great for toning your body + feeling strong!! Simply search up a workout on Youtube and have fun :)

  18. Creating a flower bouquet is such a beautiful way to decorate your house and also have some fun! Simply just arrange some flowers, and create a beautiful bouquet to display in your house <33

  19. Knitting is such a fun hobby and a great way to pass time! You can knit scarfs, hats, shirts, and more!

  20. Watching a cooking show is such a fun way to relax and get some recipe ideas! There are so many shows such as Final Table, Sugar Rush, and more!!
